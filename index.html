Simple Initiative Tracker

For Dungeons & Dragons (Or your tabletop game of choice)

Enter a new character:


Name Dex. Mod Initiative
Example: Mark 2 13

Instructions:

Enter a character's name, initiative roll, and dexterity modifier (to settle ties in initiative). Press the "Start the Encounter" button to sort the table.

Notes: If initative is left blank, a random number from 1-20 will be selected. (Dexterity mod will still be 0, however.)
You can also create multiple creatures of the same type; they will be differentiated when the table is sorted.
(e.g. "Goblin", "Goblin" -> "Goblin A", "Goblin B")
